## Small Studios, Big Challenges
### Indie (independent) studios operate far from massive corporate systems, giving them the freedom to experiment, innovate, and tell stories on their own terms.
### Why Indie Is Not Always So Great:
* Independent Funding
** Limited budgets mean creativity often depends on crowdfunding or fan support.
* Smaller Scale
** Team members wear many hats, balancing multiple roles to get the work done.
### BUT
### Why Indie Is So Great 
* Creative control - Freedom to tell unique, bold stories without corporate interference.
* Flexibility - Ability to experiment and pivot quickly.
* Varied Formats - Shorts, features, digital series, and more.
* Fueled by Passion - Driven by love for the craft, not profit margins.
### Even with small teams and tight budgets, they create stories full of: Heart, Originality, Vision.
